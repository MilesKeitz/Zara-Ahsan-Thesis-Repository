Hey, I'm Charlie Baker. I grew up in Needham, Massachusetts. I currently live in Swampscott with my wife and our three kids, and I'm a candidate for governor.
I want Massachusetts to be great. And when I say great, what I mean is I want everybody who wants to work to be able to find a job and keep it. I want every school to be a great school, and I want every community to be a great place — a thriving place where people believe tomorrow is going to be better than today, from one end of the Commonwealth to the other.
That's what I think a great Massachusetts is all about.
